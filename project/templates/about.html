Connecting Singapore's Culture

One platform. Two databases. Infinite possibilities.

The Challenge

Discovering local arts is fragmented. Official events live on government portals, while underground gigs are hidden on social media.

Result: A disconnected arts community.

The Unified Solution

We built a "Unified Feed" engine that merges official Gov.sg API data (MongoDB) with user-submitted Community Events (MySQL).

Result: Every event, big or small, on one page.

Democratizing the
Arts Scene
